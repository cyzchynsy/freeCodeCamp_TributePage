TWICE Journey Throughout The Years

  • 2015 - debuted under the company of JYP Entertainment.
  • 2016 - received their 1st daesang at 2016 MAMA by winning "Song of the Year" award for "Cheer Up"
  • 2017 - debuted in Japan and received their 2nd daesang by winning "Song of the Year" award for "Signal"
  • 2018 - they became the only kpop girl group to win 3 consecutive "Song of the Year" award by winning another daesang for "What is Love?
  • 2019 - they sold over 1 million albums in GAON
  • 2020 - surprise release of their new song "Cry for Me" and surpassed 5 million copies sold of their songs worldwide.
  • 2021 - highest first week sale for a kpop girl group for "Formula of Love: O+T = <3" which sold 318,840 copies
  • 2022 - first ever solo debut by Nayeon
  • 2023 - first kpop girl group to attend at Billboard Women.
